About

Who I Am

A Chartered Accountant who doesn't just advise — who builds, leads, and delivers.

I'm a Chartered Accountant who builds things. Most finance professionals analyse and advise — my approach is to pair domain expertise with execution. Whether it is coding an automation tool, authoring a BRD, or designing an ERP architecture, I focus on delivering tangible, working solutions to leadership.

My career has moved deliberately from audit → credit → consulting — each step adding a layer. Articleship taught me how businesses actually run. IDFC FIRST Bank taught me how risk, capital, and real money decisions get made at the C-suite level. Contetra gave me the canvas: 11+ live transformation engagements, 15-person teams, and the freedom to build from scratch.

I work across SAP ECC, SAP S/4 HANA, Oracle, and ERP Next — every one of them learned and delivered on live client projects. I cover end-to-end finance process frameworks — O2C, P2P, R2R, H2R, S2P, Q2C, T2R, A2R, and I2P — across every engagement. Now actively seeking roles in Finance Transformation, ERP Implementation, Product Management, and Management Consulting — where domain depth and hands-on technology delivery create real impact at scale.
