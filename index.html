About

Builder. Operator. Problem-Solver.

Finance gives me the business lens, technology gives me leverage, and execution turns both into operating rhythm.

I'm a CA-qualified finance and operations builder working across finance transformation, ERP/product workflows, automation, MIS, and business execution. I translate unstructured CXO priorities into operating models, dashboards, governance trackers, automation tools, and practical process changes.

I work as the bridge between CXO intent and execution: decoding business priorities, designing operating models, building dashboards and trackers, automating workflows, and aligning cross-functional teams through structured follow-ups and review mechanisms.

I work without a playbook when needed. I've managed cross-functional teams of 15+ members across finance, IT, operations, outsourced developers, and client stakeholders, serving as the primary point of contact and project lead across all engagements. I present directly to CEOs, CFOs, COOs, and founders, and I build the dashboards, trackers, and governance systems they use to run their businesses.

My work lives where finance, systems, and execution meet, across Founder's Office and CXO execution, Strategy & Operations, ERP and product transformation, finance automation, and program management. Wherever a business needs structure turned into outcomes, that's the problem I like to own.

Financial Close Automation
Schedule III · 21 branches · 15 states
Problem

Manual financial data consolidation and Schedule III notes to accounts preparation took ~15 days across 21 branches in 15 states.

Solution

Built financial statement automation using Excel, VBA, and Power Query to consolidate financial data and prepare Schedule III outputs including notes to accounts.

Outcome

Reduced monthly close time from ~15 days to under half a day across 21 branches in 15 states. Solo-built in under one month. Tool is live and has been scaled to multiple clients.

SaaS MVP · Working Prototype · In Active Development

Solution

Revenue Assurance Product
SaaS MVP · Working Prototype · In Active Development
Problem

Project-based businesses (EPC, O&M, long-cycle contracts) had no single integrated solution for revenue assurance: contract lifecycle, WBS, milestone billing, resource allocation, invoicing, and reconciliation were all handled across separate tools.

Solution

Designed end-to-end product from O&M billing diagnostic insight, covering contract lifecycle management, WBS, milestone tracking, multi-type billing, resource allocation, invoicing, payment tracking, and reconciliation. White paper → BRD → PRD → working MVP.

Outcome

Working MVP demoed and tested. Addresses a gap no standard ERP covers for project-based revenue businesses.
